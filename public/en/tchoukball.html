Tchoukball

I'm very much involved in tchoukball in Switzerland. After playing for five years at the Tchoukball Club La Chaux-de-Fonds, my studies at Swiss Institute of Technology Lausanne (EPFL) made me change club. I then played at the Nyon Tchoukball Club for six years. In September 2015, as I started working at Nothing, I joined the Tchoukball Club Bern. In September 2018, I am welcomed in the Lausanne Tchoukball Club as I moved in the area. I am also a member of the Swiss Tchoukball Executive Committee and have been a player in the Swiss national squad from 2007 to 2010.
