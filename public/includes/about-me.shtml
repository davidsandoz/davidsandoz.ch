About me

French below

I was born on December 26, 1989 in Neuchâtel, Switzerland. I did my whole schooling in La Chaux-de-Fonds, until high school at the Lycée Blaise-Cendrars. Then I studied at the Swiss Institute of Technology Lausanne (EPFL), where I accomplished a Master degree in Computer Science. Currently, I am a Software Engineer at Nothing in Wabern.

Since July 19, 2008, my heart beats for Caroline González with whom I live a fabulous story!

I'm involved a lot in tchoukball in Switzerland. After playing for five years at the Tchoukball Club La Chaux-de-Fonds, my studies at Swiss Institute of Technology Lausanne (EPFL) made me change club. I then played at the Nyon Tchoukball Club for six years. In September 2015, as I started working at Nothing, I joined the Tchoukball Club Bern. In September 2018, I am welcomed in the Lausanne Tchoukball Club as I moved in the area. I am also a member of the Swiss Tchoukball Executive Committee and have been a player in the Swiss national squad from 2007 to 2010.

Amateur photographer, I try my hand at sports photography, mainly tchoukball. You'll find my pictures on my Flickr account, but also on the one from Swiss Tchoukball. For a selection of my best shots, see you on my 500px account. From time to time, I also post on Instagram where I force myself to share only photographs taken with my iPhone.

Last update August 25, 2018
